Adjectives

Adjectives modify nouns, as in English.

Heam sur beer.
Translated: He is sexy.

Adjectivization and Regular Adjectives

Erdic is currently undergoing a shift in which most adjectives are acquiring the -t/-et suffix. Adjectives with this ending are considered regular. Nouns can generally be adjectivized to act as an adjective using the -t/-et suffix. However, this is still not the case for many common of adjectives which have not become regular.

Heam sur hoepet.
Translated: He is fatherly.
